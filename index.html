I wouldn’t log into Robinhood on your work computer unless you absolutely have to.

Main reasons:

  1. Company monitoring — work computers can track websites, screenshots, browser history, installed apps, and network activity.

  2. Security risk — your brokerage login is sensitive. Even if your company is legit, it’s not your device.

  • Log out of Robinhood on the work computer.

  • Do not save the password.

  • Clear browser history/cache for that session.

  • Check Robinhood’s logged-in devices/sessions and sign out of any you don’t recognize.

  • Make sure 2FA is on.

It’s probably not a huge disaster if you only checked it once, but going forward I’d keep Robinhood off the work computer.
