    Research method

    Process each Monday/Thursday update should follow before changing ratings.

    1

    Verify current data

    Check price, earnings, valuation multiples, balance sheet, and recent news before ratings.

    2

    Compare valuation

    Use forward P/E, FCF yield, EV/EBITDA, historical range, and peer comparisons.

    3

    Score quality

    Rank business quality, growth, margins, debt, moat, durability, and risk.

    4

    Beat the default

    Every stock must beat the case for simply adding to VOO, QQQM, MSFT, GOOGL, or NVDA.
