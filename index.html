Research method

This is the process each Monday/Thursday update should follow before giving ratings.

1

Verify current data

Check current price, recent earnings, valuation multiples, balance sheet, and relevant market news before forming an opinion.

2

Compare valuation

Use forward P/E, free cash flow yield, EV/EBITDA, historical range, and peer comparisons to judge cheap vs expensive.

3

Score quality

Rank business quality, growth, margins, debt, moat, durability, risks, and analyst expectations.

4

Beat the default

Every stock must prove why it deserves money over VOO, QQQM, MSFT, GOOGL, or NVDA.
